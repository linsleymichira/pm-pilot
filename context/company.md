## Identity

- **Operating name:** JD North America (JDNA)
- **Parent:** JD Sports Fashion plc (UK-listed; LSE: JD.) [Source: jdplc.com regulatory filings]
- **Role of JDNA:** North American operating arm consolidating JD Sports' US retail brands.

## Portfolio of brands under JDNA

- **JD** — flagship JD Sports banner; Finish Line stores are being converted to the JD fascia under the "JD First" strategy. [Source: SGB Media]
- **Finish Line** — acquired by JD Sports in 2018 for ~$558M ($13.50/share). 556 US stores + Macy's concessions at acquisition. [Source: FashionNetwork USA, Hughes Hubbard]
- **Shoe Palace** — acquired December 2020 for ~$325M. California-based, ~165 stores at acquisition. [Source: Wikipedia, Chain Store Age]
- **DTLR** (formerly DTLR/Villa) — acquired February/March 2021 for ~$495M. Baltimore-based streetwear/athletic footwear. [Source: Baird, Chain Store Age]
- **Hibbett** — acquisition announced April 2024, completed July 25 2024, ~$1.1B deal. [Source: jdplc.com, Retail Insight Network]
- **Nice Kicks** — [Assumption] sits within JDNA portfolio per PM input; confirm exact ownership structure.
- **City Gear** — owned by JD Sports in US market. [Source: FashionNetwork USA]

## Acquisition timeline (US entry)

- **Jun 2018** — Finish Line acquisition completed (~$558M)

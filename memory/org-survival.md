**Power level:** High. Runs People/HR across all JDNA brands (Finish Line, Hibbett, Shoe Palace, DTLR). Two levels above user (user → Jacob Gray → Seve).

**Direct reports (confirmed 2026-04-24) — four arms:**
- **Jackie Gessner** (VP, Talent Mgmt & HR Compliance) — talent / compliance / comp-philosophy + People BP arm. Owns Jenny Pund (→ April + Sean Smyth), Blair Orlansky, Sydney Purichia (→ Mya + Kim), Hannah Reeser.
- **Jacob Gray** (Compensation) — comp mechanics / pay-band arm. Owns the user (Linsley).
- **Chad Leathers** (Sr. Director, People Operations) — People Tech / Workday / analytics infrastructure arm. Owns Jacob Amick (→ Rosa Guzman) and William Dou.
- **Macaira O'Connell** (Manager, Communications and Employee Engagement) — single-person strategic arm covering comms + engagement.

**What they want:**
- Cross-brand HR/comp consolidation — a single JDNA system of record (retail pay bands, KF Pay, comp processes).
- A People org that shows up as a unified function post-acquisitions, not as 4+ legacy brands with their own practices.
- HR Scorecard — standardized people metrics (recruitment, engagement, turnover, succession) across JDNA. He sponsors; Jackie drives; Macaira O'Connell executes.
- [Assumption] BI/analytics that spans brands, not FL-only.

**Risk pattern:**
- Cancels 1:1s under pressure (observed: 2 in 10 days, Apr 10 + Apr 20, 2026) — cadence can erode silently unless the direct report protects it.
- Delegates operationally; won't ask for status updates — stale work on a direct report's plate can go invisible to him until much later.
- Dual email-domain persona (`finishline.com` / `jdnorthamerica.com` / `jdplc.com`) means a reply to the wrong address can land in the wrong audience context.

**Approach:**
- Bring updates proactively; don't wait for him to ask. Assume silence = he's busy, not uninterested.
- Frame work in cross-brand terms when it's defensible. Single-brand framing is lower-leverage to him.
- Protect 1:1 cadence — if he cancels 2+ in a row, offer an explicit re-plan (biweekly? async check-in?) so it doesn't drift.
- Match his domain context on replies (jdna-wide reply → `jdnorthamerica.com`; FL-specific → `finishline.com`).
